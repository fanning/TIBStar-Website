Ship Classes

Fighter

Role: Fast, agile combat ship

Strengths: High speed and maneuverability, good for hit-and-run tactics

Weaknesses: Lower armor and cargo capacity

Cruiser

Role: Balanced combat and utility

Strengths: Well-rounded stats, versatile equipment options

Weaknesses: Not specialized in any particular role

Battleship

Role: Heavy combat ship

Strengths: High armor and firepower, excellent tank

Weaknesses: Slower movement speed

Harvester

Role: Resource gathering

Strengths: Large cargo hold, efficient resource collection

Weaknesses: Limited combat capabilities

Equipment Types

Weapons

  • Lasers: Energy-based weapons

  • Railguns: Long-range precision

Defense

Resources & Crafting

Resources are collected from asteroids, debris, and defeated enemies. Use resources to craft equipment, repair your ship, and purchase upgrades at stations.

Common Resources

Iron, Copper, Silicon - Found in low-level sectors, used for basic equipment and repairs.

Rare Resources

Titanium, Platinum, Iridium - Found in higher-level sectors, used for advanced equipment.

Special Materials

Exotic matter, Dark matter - Rare drops from bosses and special events, used for legendary equipment.

Game Mechanics

Experience & Leveling

Gain experience by defeating enemies, completing missions, and harvesting resources. Each level increases your ship's base stats and unlocks access to higher-tier equipment.

Corporations

Join a corporation to team up with other players, share resources, and participate in corporate warfare. Corporations can control sectors and build stations.

PvP Combat

Player vs Player combat is enabled in certain sectors. Low-level sectors (1-10) are protected. Be prepared to defend yourself in higher-level space.

Sectors & Navigation

The galaxy is divided into sectors with different level ranges. Higher-level sectors contain tougher enemies but better rewards. Use the sector map to navigate.

Missions & Quests

Accept missions from stations or NPCs. Complete objectives for credits, experience, and equipment rewards. Daily missions provide bonus rewards.

Economy

Credits

The primary currency in TIB. Earned from missions, selling items, and defeating enemies. Used to purchase equipment and services.

Black Dollars

Premium currency that can be purchased with real money. Used for cosmetic items, convenience features, and special equipment.

Trading

Trade items and resources with other players. Use the trade window to ensure safe transactions.
