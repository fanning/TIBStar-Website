Tutorial - Getting Started

Welcome to The Infinite Black

Learn the basics and start your journey through the infinite cosmos

Getting Started

  1. Create Your Account
    Launch the game and create a new account. Choose your commander name wisely - this will be your identity in the galaxy!
  2. Select Private Server
    When prompted, select "Private Server" to connect to the official TIB servers.
  3. Learn the Controls
    Use the left side of the screen to move your ship. Tap enemies to target and attack them automatically.
  4. Complete Your First Missions
    Follow the initial tutorial missions to learn combat, navigation, and equipment management.

Basic Controls

Movement

Touch and hold on the left side of the screen to move your ship. Your ship will continue moving in that direction.

Combat

Tap on an enemy ship or NPC to target it. Your weapons will automatically fire when in range. Tap again to de-select.

Harvesting

Equip a harvester and fly near asteroids or debris. Tap the object to begin harvesting resources.

Menu

Access the game menu from the top of the screen to view your ship, inventory, missions, and more.

Core Gameplay

Leveling Up

Defeat enemies and complete missions to gain experience. Each level increases your ship's capabilities and unlocks new equipment.

Equipment

Find and equip weapons, armor, shields, and other items to improve your ship. Higher level equipment provides better stats and abilities.

Resources

Collect resources from asteroids and defeated enemies. Use these resources to craft items, repair your ship, and purchase upgrades.

Multiplayer

The Infinite Black is a multiplayer game. Join a corporation, team up with other players, or engage in PvP combat in designated areas.

Tips for New Players

  • Stay in safe zones initially: Low-level sectors are protected from PvP combat. Learn the game before venturing into dangerous space.
  • Join a corporation: Playing with others makes the game more fun and helps you learn faster.
  • Complete daily missions: Daily missions provide good rewards and help you progress quickly.
  • Upgrade regularly: Don't hoard equipment. Use better items as soon as you find them.
  • Ask for help: The TIB community is generally helpful. Don't hesitate to ask questions in chat.
