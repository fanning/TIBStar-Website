Community Resources

In-Game Chat

The best way to connect with the TIB community is through the in-game chat system. Join the global chat channel to meet other players, ask questions, and coordinate activities.

Corporation Chat

Join a corporation to access private corporation chat channels. This is ideal for coordinating with your team and building lasting friendships.

Tips for Community Interaction

  • Be respectful: Treat other players with courtesy and respect
  • Ask questions: The community is generally helpful to new players
  • Share knowledge: Help others when you can and share useful tips
  • Team up: Many activities are more fun with friends
  • Report issues: Help improve the game by reporting bugs and problems

Finding Players

How to find other players in-game

Use the sector scanner to see other players in your current sector. Higher-level sectors tend to have more active players. Popular gathering spots include major stations and resource-rich areas.

Joining a Corporation

Check the corporation list in-game to browse active corporations. Many corporations recruit new members regularly. Look for one that matches your play style and schedule.

Making Friends

Add players to your friends list to stay connected. You can see when friends are online and coordinate activities more easily.
